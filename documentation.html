Player Arrays

Each player has an array (one-row list) attributed to them in order to yield and pull information about the players. The array will be modified throughout the game to reflect the player's stats and the deck.

Here's a pic of the beginning of the init for the class Player:

The following table shows the indices of the value, the standard value, and the meaning of each value.

Array Index Standard Values Description
0 deck(n) The deck of player n, itself a realtime list.
1 playedcards(n) Realtime list of played cards for player N.
2 remainingcards(n) Realtime list of remaining cards. Different for every player due to imperfect information.
3 0, 1, 2 Nil: False, True, blind
4 p: 0, 1, 2 Partner nil: False, True, blind
5 X Tricks taken
6 X Bid
7 p: X Partner tricks taken
8 p: x Partner bid
9 XX Team tricks taken
10 XX Team bid
11 XX Enemy team tricks
12 XX Enemy team bid
13 XXX Total team bags
14 XXX Total enemy bags
15 XXX Team score
16 XXX Enemey team score
17 XNX AI=0, PLAYER=1
